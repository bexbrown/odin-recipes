Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Butter
  • Baking Powder
  • Chocolate Chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Mash bananas until smooth.
  2. Mix in eggs, milk, vanilla and softened butter
  3. Add the remaining dry ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Pour batter into a baking pan.
  6. Bake at 350Â°C for 20 mins or until golden brown.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
