Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sour Cream
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Cheese
  • Additional toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine yeast and warm water in bowl.
  2. Add flour, milk and salt to yeast mix and knead until dough ball forms.
  3. Cover dough and set dough aside for 30 mins.
  4. Mix together tomato sauce, sour cream, diced garlic and oregano.
  5. When dough has risen, roll out on a pan as thin as desired.
  6. Spread a thin layer of sauce and top with cheese and additional toppings.
  7. Cook at 425Â°C for 10 mins or until cheese is golden brown.
  8. Serve immediatley.
