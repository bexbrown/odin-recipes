Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Spinach
  • Bell Pepper
  • Salt and Peppern(to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef until browned.
  2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 min).
  3. Add tomato sauce and diced bell pepper to beef.
  4. Steam spinach and mix with Ricotta cheese
  5. Start assembling lasagna with a layer of the cheese and spinach mix on bottom of cassarole dish.
  6. Layer lasagna noodles, beef mix then cheese until finished.
  7. Top with Parmesan Cheese
  8. Cook at 425Â°C for 40 mins or until lasagna is bubbling and golden brown.
  9. Serve immediatley
